ABOUT

Wander

Wander is a simple app that keeps track of your favorite photos and travel destinations. Do you love to travel? Then this is the app for you!

Travel

Do you love to travel? Do you want to share your favorite travel destinations, stories, and photos with friends? Keep track and continue wandering!

Passport

Your passport holds all your favorite photos and travel destinations in one place. Keep stamping your passport and add more and more destinations, keeping track of some of your favorite memories!
